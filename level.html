ART & CRAFT

Arts and crafts are forms of creative expression that involve making objects or artworks by hand. This can include everything from painting and drawing to sculpture, woodworking, pottery, and textile art.

Arts and crafts play an essential role in modern problem-solving as they promote creativity and innovation. The ability to think outside the box and come up with unique solutions is increasingly valuable in today's world. Artistic and creative pursuits can help individuals develop these skills and apply them to various fields, including business, technology, and science.

Moreover, arts and crafts offer a wide range of future opportunities. As the world becomes more automated and digitized, the demand for handcrafted goods and unique, artisanal products is increasing. There is a growing interest in the "maker" movement, which emphasizes the importance of handmade goods, and the demand for customized, personalized items is on the rise.

Additionally, the arts and crafts industry plays a significant role in promoting cultural heritage and preserving traditional techniques and knowledge. By preserving and promoting these techniques, future generations can continue to benefit from the unique insights and perspectives of previous generations.

How To Participate

Welcome to the National STEAM Olympiad 2023! We are thrilled to have you participate in this exciting competition. Below are the guidelines for the Quiz and Project categories in the first round, which will be held online:

Quiz:

  • The quiz will consist of multiple-choice questions related to STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics).
  • Participants will have 30 minutes to complete the quiz, and the questions will cover a wide range of topics related to STEAM.
  • The quiz will be held online, and participants will need a stable internet connection and a computer or mobile device to access the platform.
  • The quiz platform will be provided by the organizers, and participants will receive instructions on how to access it.

Project:

  • Participants will need to submit a project proposal online, which should include a title, description, and methodology of the project.
  • The proposal should be no longer than 500 words and should clearly explain the problem the project is trying to solve and how it will be addressed.
  • The proposal should also include any necessary diagrams or images to illustrate the project idea.
  • The project proposal should be submitted before the deadline provided by the organizers.

For the second round, selected participants will be invited to participate in division-wise offline events in their respective divisions. The division-wise events will also consist of quiz and project categories, and participants will be evaluated based on their performance in both categories.

For the final round, the top participants from each division will be invited to participate in a centrally-held event in Dhaka. The final round will consist of quiz and project categories as well, and participants will be evaluated based on their performance in both categories.
